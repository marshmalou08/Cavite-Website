Lights, Flavors, and Finds: The Night Market Experience

Somo Market | PUROK 138, 3VVX+5F8 Barangay, Neogan, Tagaytay City, Cavite

Food tastes better with a view—and in Tagaytay, you get front-row seats to one of the most iconic views in the Philippines: the stunning Taal Volcano. From cozy cafés to elegant restaurants serving a diverse range of cuisine, Tagaytay has perfected the art of turning every meal into a scenic experience. Here are Tagaytay Restaurants with a view of Taal Volcano you should visit to elevate your dining experience.

Read more ->




Agapita Cafe

3WXW+X75, Tagaytay, Cavite

This restaurant gives total probinsya vibes with its cute Kubo-style huts and wooden tables — the perfect mix of warm and rustic. Plus, you get an incredible view of Taal Volcano, so get your cameras ready, because every corner is Instagram-worthy. They also serve authentic Filipino favorites, including Bulalo, Sinigang na Baboy, Sisig, and many more.

Read more ->






Somo Market

Tagaytay – Nasugbu Hwy, Tagaytay City, 4120 Cavite

Balay Dako literally means “Big House” in Negrense — and it totally lives up to the name. This place looks straight out of an old Filipino movie, with vintage paintings, chandeliers, and balconies overlooking Taal Lake. They serve classic Filipino dishes, and while it’s a bit pricey, the servings are massive, so it’s totally worth it. Don’t skip the garden area, too — it’s perfect for photos with a backdrop of the breathtaking Taal Volcano view.
